Become a Guardian

Choose from three distinct classes, each with its own strengths and abilities.

Titan

The frontline warrior. Masters of defense and brute strength. Titans use their strength to protect allies and crush enemies.

  • Striker: Unleash powerful arc-based attacks.
  • Sentinel: Defend your team with void shields.
  • Sunbreaker: Wield flaming hammers of destruction.

Warlock

Harness the power of the Light and use it to support your allies. Warlocks are the mystics of the battlefield, wielding arcane energy.

  • Dawnblade: Soar through the air with flaming swords.
  • Voidwalker: Channel deadly void energy.
  • Stormcaller: Unleash powerful lightning attacks.

Hunter

Quick, agile, and deadly. Masters of precision and movement. Hunters are the scouts of the Last City, striking with deadly efficiency.

  • Gunslinger: Use golden gun to incinerate enemies.
  • Arcstrider: Dance through battle with a staff of pure arc energy.
  • Nightstalker: Utilize void energy to trap enemies.
