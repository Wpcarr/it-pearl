Game Modes

Whether you prefer PvE challenges, PvP combat, or the hybrid chaos of Gambit, Destiny 2 offers thrilling activities for every Guardian.

Player vs Environment (PvE)

Engage in cooperative missions, take down powerful bosses, and explore the mysteries of the universe.

  • Strikes: Short cooperative missions with intense combat.
  • Dungeons: Challenging multi-phase encounters for fireteams.
  • Raids: High-stakes six-player challenges with complex mechanics.
  • Onslaught: A fast-paced PvE mode with waves of enemies and time-based challenges for rewards.

Player vs Player (PvP)

Prove your skills against other Guardians in various competitive modes.

  • Control: Team-based zone control combat.
  • Elimination: Tactical 3v3 battles.
  • Trials of Osiris: High-stakes competitive PvP.
  • Iron Banner: A PvP event with power-level advantages and exclusive rewards.

Gambit

A unique hybrid mode where PvE and PvP collide in a battle for motes and control.

  • Collect Motes: Defeat enemies and deposit motes.
  • Summon Primeval: Bank 100 motes to summon the final boss.
  • Invade: Attack the opposing team to slow their progress.

Seasonal Activity

Each season introduces a unique activity with new challenges, rewards, and story developments.

  • New Storylines: Expanding the universe with lore-driven content.
  • Exclusive Loot: Earn powerful seasonal weapons and armor.
  • Limited-Time Events: Participate in time-sensitive activities for special rewards.

Holiday Events

Destiny 2 celebrates special occasions with themed events, activities, and rewards.

  • Festival of the Lost: Halloween-themed event with haunted sectors and cosmetics.
  • The Dawning: Winter event featuring gift-giving and special quests.
  • Guardian Games: Class-based competition with unique rewards.
