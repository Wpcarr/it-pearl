Game Modes

PvE (Player vs Environment)

  • Strikes: Three-player missions against powerful enemies.
  • Raids: Six-player high-difficulty missions with puzzles and bosses.
  • Dungeons: Three-player mini-raids with unique mechanics.

PvP (Player vs Player)

  • Crucible: Classic multiplayer battles.
  • Gambit: Hybrid PvE/PvP mode.
  • Trials of Osiris: High-stakes 3v3 competitive mode.
